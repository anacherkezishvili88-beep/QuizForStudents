CONCEPT

This project is a quiz/tool made for students who have moved out of their homes and are building new lives for themselves in university.

I have personal experience with moving to a whole different country at the age of 17 and I've also have had very deep insight into my peers' and friends' experiences with moving out for college, so I based this quiz on our experiences and how we navigate our new lives.

My aim with this project was to offer fellow students a place for them to reflect and understand what comforts them, how they regulate certain emotions, and how they want to shape their college experience.
